WHY STUDY WITH KAPLAN

We're your first step to a bigger world

Kaplan is a trusted provider of excellence in education.

We have over 75 years of teaching experience, helping millions of students improve their career prospects.

Our philosophy is not to limit your education to classrooms and textbooks, but to enjoy the benefits of a fully immersive experience where you can continue to learn English outside of class, and with our specially developed K+ curriculum you’ll quickly advance in speaking, writing, and reading English.

Teaching is our passion. We take great pride in ensuring that all our students have access to the best tools and opportunities to succeed when they leave our doors.

You'll also love being able to explore new countries, expereince different cultures, gain lifelong friends and memories.
